FAQ

Everything you need to know before starting

Got questions? Here are answers to some common ones clients ask.

How long does a typical website project take?

Most projects are completed within 1–3 weeks, depending on complexity and client feedback turnaround. We follow an agile development process with clear milestones:

  • Discovery & Planning: 2-3 days
  • Design Phase: 3-5 days
  • Development: 5-10 days
  • Testing & Revisions: 2-3 days
Do you work with Figma designs or create custom designs?

We offer both options to suit your needs:

  • Figma to WordPress: We specialize in converting Figma mockups into pixel-perfect, fully responsive WordPress websites with attention to every design detail.
  • Custom Design: Our design team can create a unique, brand-aligned design system tailored to your business goals and user needs.
Will my website be mobile-responsive and performant?

Absolutely. We adhere to modern web standards to ensure:

  • Fully responsive design across all device sizes
  • 90+ Lighthouse performance scores
  • Optimized assets and lazy loading
  • Progressive enhancement strategy

All websites include performance testing reports before delivery.

Why do some portfolio sites look different from their original state?

Some portfolio items represent work completed during my tenure at a digital marketing agency. These projects may have evolved since my involvement:

  • Client Modifications: Some clients made subsequent changes that affected design integrity
  • Third-Party Updates: Plugin or platform updates sometimes introduce compatibility issues
  • Maintenance: Without ongoing maintenance, even well-built sites can degrade over time

All original deliverables met strict quality standards for design, responsiveness, and performance.
